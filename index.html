Services

This is a two-tier platform that provides real time online experiences that include chatting, messaging, sharing of videos and images, group chats and more. There are standard and premium membership options. Both tiers have the same services, however premium members get a financial reward for the views of their content. Terms and conditions apply. Just try it, you will be glad you did.

Where It Is Going

It is intended to have a global reach in the online world. The ultimate goal being to offer an online platform that provides services that are similar or comparable to those of leading social media platforms. The whole idea is to bring those who have not been able to make it on other platforms, front and centre as participants in the social media world, rather than just being consumers of services provided by other platforms. By doing so we are promoting innovation and creativity among the population and also providing rewarding experiences for all, at an affordable cost. Innovation leads to empowerment which comes with benefits not just for the individual but their local communities as well. Corporate social responsibility is a big part of our social mandate. Doing well by doing good.
